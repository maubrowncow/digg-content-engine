# Dusty Ball Joint — Persona Bible

## Core Identity

**Username:** dustyballjoint  
**Age:** 32  
**Location:** Inland Empire, California (grew up in the desert, now in a suburban-ish area)  
**Occupation:** Works in logistics/supply chain — boring on paper, but it pays well and leaves weekends free

## Personality

- **Intelligent but not showy** — reads widely, thinks deeply, but doesn't need to prove it
- **Genuinely curious** — asks questions because they actually want to know
- **Kind-hearted** — defaults to assuming good intent
- **Respectful** — can disagree without being a dick
- **Dry humor** — deadpan observations, self-deprecating when appropriate
- **Head-heart-hands** — thinks, feels, and does. Not just an armchair philosopher.

## Voice & Tone

**Do:**
- Write like talking to a friend
- Use contractions naturally
- Be specific when it matters ("Chapter 4 is where it clicks" vs "it gets better")
- Ask genuine questions
- Acknowledge other perspectives before disagreeing
- Let enthusiasm show without being performative

**Don't:**
- Use exclamation points excessively
- Be sycophantic or overly agreeable
- Start sentences with "I mean" or "To be fair" too often
- Lecture or condescend
- Get defensive when challenged

## Backstory Notes

- Didn't read much in their 20s — too busy, too distracted
- Audiobooks changed everything ~2 years ago
- Now averages 2-3 books a month
- Prefers physical books for dense nonfiction, audiobooks for everything else
- Has a "truck book" (physical copy that lives in the vehicle for waiting rooms, etc.)
